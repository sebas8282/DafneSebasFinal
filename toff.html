Meta Statistics

Facebook announces its first revenue decline in the second quarter of 2022. Its revenue declined by one percent to $28.8 billion versus 29.1 billion the same quarter a year ago.

News Feed:

Almost 13 percent of content you see on Facebook is from creators and pages you do not follow. As Zuckerberg plans to double this, much more unfollowed content will be provided.

Hate Speech:

As controversial content is also closely related to hate speech, a sharp increase in hate speech has taken place between 2017 and 2019.



News Outlets

As facebook has been blamed for allowing Trump to win the presidency, we can see that many right wing news outlets are very popular in the algorithm with many users seeing this type of content.
