Case Study

Our case study focuses on how Zuckerburg (Meta) plans to increase content we don't follow on his social media apps. Zuckerberg's plan is to increase the algorithm within the apps to recommend new content. As Tik Tok becomes an increasing threat to Meta and their views within the app, they must find new ways to push content in order to keep users on their apps.
